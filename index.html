Frequently Asked Questions

The capital of France is Paris. It's renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture.

There are eight planets in our solar system: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Pluto was reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006.

"To Kill a Mockingbird" was written by Harper Lee. The novel was published in 1960 and was an immediate success, winning the Pulitzer Prize.
